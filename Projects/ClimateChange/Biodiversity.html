Climate Change and Biodiversity




Climate Change has a negitive impact with Biodiversity because...


• Biodiversity is failing because we force animals to move to cooler places like mountains or migrate north.
By making animals move to a different habitat, they will need to find a new food cycle or die of hunger.


• If climate change continues and temperatures increase, Plants will also take a toll. Since plants do not have nerves,
they can’t move meaning that if climate change continues, plants will die.


• If plants die, we will need to rethink our foods and plant-based food like grain and rice will be much more expensive.
If common food is going to go up, our economy will fall too. Think about it, without being able to buy Fruits, Vegetables and Grains,
our economy can turn dangerous.
